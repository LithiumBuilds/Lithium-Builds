BUILT BY ENTHUSIASTS

Lithium Builds is a Sydney-based custom PC business run by a father and son team who've been building computers for years. We got tired of seeing people pay too much for bad pre-builts, so we started doing it ourselves — properly.

We're not a warehouse. We're not a faceless online store. We're two people who genuinely care about the machines we put our name on. Every cable is managed. Every part is tested. Every customer gets treated like they matter — because to us, they do.

Three tiers for every budget: current-gen builds for enthusiasts, solid all-new machines for smart buyers, and honest budget builds using a mix of new and quality-tested used parts.
